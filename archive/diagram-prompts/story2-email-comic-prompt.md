# Story 2: Email Response Comic Strip

A 3-panel comic strip showing how Conscious Insights helps Juan respond thoughtfully to a difficult email about his team's performance.

---

## Story Arc

**Trigger:** Juan receives a challenging email questioning his team's performance
**Intervention:** CI sidebar in Outlook helps him think through his response
**Outcome:** He sends a thoughtful reply instead of reacting defensively
