## Commercial Framing (Oseas's Reframe)

### The Problem

> "Veo blockers comerciales para que la compañía diga 'compro coaching'... y luego mi visión del ser humano de que somos horribles para cambiar."

Individual coaching alone is hard to sell:
- No budget category
- Compliance blockers
- Low behavior change adherence when solo

### The Reframe

> "Cambia mucho la manera de poder abordar la cuestión comercial si el framing desde el principio es que tenemos una solución que se llama Team Performance."

**Sell Team Performance, deliver individual coaching.**

The individual coaching becomes:
- Part of responsibility to team (not personal development)
- Social accountability (team is watching)
- Connected to team diagnostic (not abstract)

### Why This Helps Adherence

> "Una de las cosas que ayudan a la adhesión del cambio de comportamiento es el entorno social... Si estamos todos en equipo haciéndolo, está un poquito más cabrón."

Social context increases behavior change adherence.

## Quick Win MVP

### The Combination

| Component | Source | Status |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **Team Survey** (4-Quadrant) | Axialent internal | Exists — just needs digital form |
| **CB Assessment** | Already in product | Exists |
| **Cross-analysis** | Show how individual patterns contribute to team issues | Needs design, not coding |
| **AI Coach** | Thinking Partner modified | Exists — needs team context |
| **Facilitation** | Horacio | Human-led for now |

### How It Works

1. Team takes Team Survey → WHERE team is broken
2. Individuals take CB Assessment → HOW each contributes
3. Cross-reference: "Your team is weak on X, you're contributing via Y"
4. AI Coach guides individual work in context of team goals
5. Dashboard shows team progress (adoption + topics)

### What's NOT Needed for Prototype

> "Si la gente copia y pega los resultados de sus assessments, ni siquiera necesito a Mike."

- Can prototype with forms + copy-paste
- Leo can sell
- Horacio facilitates
- Minimal dev needed initially
